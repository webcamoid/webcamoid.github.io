Webcamoid is a multi-platform camera capture app focused on providing all major features required by power users with a very simple and intuitive interface.

Features

  • Cross-platform (GNU/Linux, Mac, Windows, Android and FreeBSD)
  • Take pictures and record videos with your camera.
  • Add funny video effects.
  • Translated to many languages.
  • Use pictures and videos as sources.
  • Screen capture support.
  • Many video recording formats.
  • Virtual camera support for feeding other programs (GNU/Linux, Mac, Windows)
