With the need of keeping the project financially up you now have many more options for making a financial contribution to the project.

Direct Donations

You can donate money directly from any of the supported sites.

  • Github Sponsors
  • Paypal

  • Ko-fi

  • Buy me a coffee

Crypto currencies donations

All most popular cryptocurrencies are supported. Again, check donations page for getting the full list.

Pre-compiled packages

You can save your time and the hustle of compiling Webcamoid your self and get a ready to use binary package for your system for a very small and accessible price. Visit the Webcamoid online store for downloading them.

Become a sponsor of the project and get early access to new Webcamoid features with the daily builds!

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: The project already accept donations, why paid options?

A: More ways of collaborating financially with the project is much better, also you get exclusive things for you for a very affordable price.

Q: It's possible to get binary packages for free?

A: Yes, of course! just download the source code and follow de compile instructions. Also, you can download it from a third-party site, just make sure you download it from a trusted site and keep you antivirus updated.
